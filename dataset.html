Road Crossing Assistant

Dataset Description

  1. 76 videos from diverse locations, time, traffic patterns, collected using an action camera on different roads of Anand, Gujarat.
  2. Video format: MOV, Resolution: 1920*1080,
    Framerate: 30 fps, Duration: 5-8 secs
  3. Annotated each video with a time instance when the road is safe to cross and the instance when the safe duration ends.
  4. Download link: (github repository link)
