Road Crossing Assistant


Dataset Description


  • 1. Videos from diverse locations, time, traffic patterns, safe/unsafe using an action camera on different roads of Anand, Gujarat.
  • 2. 76 videos of duration 5-8 seconds each.
  • 3. Annotated each video with a time instance when the road is safe to cross and the instance when the safe duration ends.
