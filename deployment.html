Road Crossing Assistant

Deployment


For final working prototype of Road Crossing Assistant which can be used by blind people in real time, we have deployed our pre-trained deep learning model on Nvidia Jetson Nano B01.

Hardware Components used
  1. Nvidia Jetson Nano B01
  2. Camera module (Raspberry Pi V2 – 8MP Camera)
  3. 64 GB UHS-1 MicroSD Card for storage
  4. USB audio adapter and headphones

Here, we have simply connected camera, audio adapter, power input and cooling fan to Nvidia Jetson Nano.

Deployment workflow


We converted our pretrained TensorFlow model into a TensorRT model to optimize the processing power and achieve low latency and high throughput needed for running the deep learning models on devices like Jetson Nano.
Jetson Nano will run the pre-trained TensorRT model on the real-time video stream (obtained from the camera module attached to it) and predict whether it is safe to cross the road seen in the video stream. It will also control the audio output of headphones to communicate with the user through simple audio commands.
