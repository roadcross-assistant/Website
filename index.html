Road Crossing Assistant

Abstract

Computer Vision is unlocking unprecedented rates of progress in all aspects of life - be it healthcare, defence, entertainment or autonomous vehicles. We are aiming to use Computer Vision to help blind or aged people for crossing roads, more precisely Indian roads where there may not be many traffic signals, and where traffic rules and speed limits may not be followed strictly. For the same, we have collected a dataset of videos capturing various traffic patterns on roads of Anand, Gujarat. We propose a ML/DL based model to predict safe duration for crossing road from the given video. As a future scope, if we get a model with good accuracy, we plan to integrate this model into a wearable assistant so that blind/aged people can use it for crossing roads in realtime.
